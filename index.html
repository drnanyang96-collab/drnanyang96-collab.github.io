About Me

PhD candidate working across orthodontics and cognitive science.

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Orthodontics at Kyushu University Hospital, Japan.

My current research focuses on the Sense of Agency (SoA) during continuous motor control. I am particularly interested in how humans evaluate self-control and authorship during dynamic interactions.

In addition to cognitive science research, I am also interested in clinical orthodontics and translational research.

Research

Research Theme

Characteristics of cues influencing the sense of agency in continuous motor control

The sense of agency refers to the feeling that one is controlling one's own actions and their consequences.

My research investigates how temporal characteristics of control disturbances influence retrospective agency judgments during continuous motor control tasks.
